# Optimization Proposal: Merrick Tire Center

## Executive Summary
The deal's strongest asset is a genuinely competitive price ($160 vs. NYC average $231) backed by a 4.5-star rating with 79% five-star reviews mentioning transparency and honest pricing. Its biggest weakness is a critical refrigerant ambiguity: the deal covers R-134a only, yet vehicles 2015+ commonly require R-1234yf ($200–$400+ per service), creating a near-certain post-purchase complaint pipeline. The single highest-leverage fix is adding a refrigerant compatibility notice and vehicle eligibility callout above the fold — this reduces refund requests, protects merchant rating, and eliminates the top complaint vector before it compounds.

---

## 1. Title Rewrite _(High Impact)_
**Current:** Complete A/C Repair with Freon and Recharge Included at Merrick Tire Center (Up to 52% Off)
**Proposed:** A/C Recharge + Vacuum Service with R-134a Freon at Merrick Tire Center — $160 (Save $71 vs. NYC Average)
**Why:** "Repair" implies fault diagnosis, which the deal may not cover — misaligned expectations drive negative reviews. Anchoring to the $231 NYC average (verified market data) replaces a credibility-challenged $300 "original price" with a defensible, honest value frame.
