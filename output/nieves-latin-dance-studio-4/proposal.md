# Optimization Proposal: Nieves Latin Dance Studio

## Executive Summary
This deal's strongest asset is exceptional social proof — 4.9/5 across 718 reviews with 92% five-star — yet the page fails to leverage it meaningfully in copy. The biggest weakness is critical undisclosed information (eligible locations, new-student restriction, class cycle start rules) that creates post-purchase friction and trust erosion. The single highest-leverage fix is surfacing the true $/class value math alongside competitor benchmarks, which instantly reframes the $35–$40 price as a near-absurd bargain rather than a vague "60% off" claim.

---

## 1. Title Rewrite _(High Impact)_
**Current:** Enjoy Fun Salsa Classes at Nieves Latin Dance Studio: 4 or Unlimited Salsa/Bachata Classes for 1 Month. Save Up to 60%

**Proposed:** Nieves Latin Dance Studio: 4 Salsa/Bachata Classes ($8.75 each) or Unlimited Classes for 1 Month — Rated 4.9★ by 700+ Students

**Why:** "Fun" is filler; the $/class breakdown ($8.75 vs. NYC market rate of $20–$35/class drop-in) does the selling. Adding the rating in the title exploits the 92% five-star signal before the page even loads.

## 2. Pricing & Value Framing _(High Impact)_
**Problem:** "Save up to 60%" is abstract and the $100 "original price" for unlimited lacks credibility given Nieves' own site implies ~$120/month retail.

**Fix — add a value callout block directly beneath the price options:**

> **4 Classes — $35** (~$8.75/class)
> NYC drop-in average: $20–$35/class. You're paying less than half.
>
> **Unlimited Month — $40**
> Comparable NYC unlimited memberships run $100–$175/month (BAILA Society, Ballet Hispanico-tier studios). This voucher pays for itself after 2 classes.

**Rationale:** Anchoring to named market comparables (BAILA Society ~$25–$35/class; Ballet Hispanico $22.78/class on a 9-class series) makes the discount concrete and credible rather than relying on a questioned $100 original price.
