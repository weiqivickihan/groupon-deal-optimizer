## Content Gaps

### 🔴 High Priority
- **No exact service inclusions listed for 'IN/OUT Premium Car Wash'** — Customers cannot determine what 'IN/OUT' means (interior + exterior? specific steps?), what products are used, or how it differs from a basic wash. This ambiguity increases pre-purchase hesitation.
- **No before/after photos on the deal page** — Visual proof of quality is the top conversion driver for detailing services. The merchant has TikTok before/after content but it is absent from the Groupon page where purchase decisions are made.
- **No mention of exact address or directions on the deal page** — The merchant is located in Bridgeview, IL — a suburb about 12 miles southwest of downtown Chicago. Chicago-area Groupon shoppers may not realize the location is outside the city proper, leading to surprise or redemption drop-off.
- **No appointment/scheduling process explained** — Customers need to know whether they must call, book online, or walk in — and what wait times to expect. Absence of this information is a top complaint category for car wash Groupon deals.

### 🟡 Medium Priority
- **No cancellation or rescheduling policy** — The 120-day repurchase restriction and 3-per-person limit suggests the deal has tight constraints, but no guidance is given for what happens if a customer needs to cancel or reschedule an appointment.
- **No clarification of what 'ceramic coating specialization' means in practice** — The highlight mentions ceramic coating expertise, but the listed deals do not include a ceramic coating service option. This creates a disconnect — customers attracted by ceramic coating may be confused that it is not in the deal.
- **No staff credentials or training information** — For a business emphasizing paint correction, ceramic coating, and premium finishes, customers want to know if technicians are certified (e.g., IDA-certified detailers). Lack of credentials reduces trust for higher-ticket upsell services.
- **No estimated service time or wait time listed** — Customers need to plan their day around a car wash visit. Without estimated duration (e.g., 45 min for a wash, 2–3 hrs for a detail), customers cannot set expectations and may be dissatisfied by longer-than-expected waits.

### ⚪ Low Priority
- **No parking or drop-off instructions** — First-time visitors to the Bridgeview location need to know lot size, whether they wait on-site, or if there is a shuttle/loaner option for longer detailing services.
- **No information on accepted payment methods or tipping norms** — Customers often want to know if tips are expected and whether cash or card is accepted beyond Groupon redemption, especially for additional services purchased on-site.
